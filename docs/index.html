AI in Construction for the built environment

This course introduces students to practical artificial intelligence workflows for construction, design, and facility operations through hands-on notebooks, datasets, sensing exercises, and student-led projects.

Overview

A clearer path into construction technology

The course is structured to be approachable for learners from construction management and adjacent fields. It balances technical depth with plain-language explanations, guided labs, and applied examples from the built environment.

Who this course serves

  • Construction management students
  • Architecture, engineering, and planning learners
  • Professionals exploring AI adoption in project delivery
  • Anyone interested in data-driven workflows for the built environment

What learners practice

  • Reading, cleaning, and visualizing construction data
  • Building machine learning and deep learning models
  • Working with sensors, computer vision, and digital twins
  • Exploring generative AI in design and decision support
