Featured invited speaker

Prof. Rui Liu

Kent State University College of Aeronautics and Engineering

AI-Powered Robot Cognition Modeling for Trustworthy Human-Robot Collaboration

Prof. Liu shared research on trust, intent, and agility modeling in robotics, and the session included a live robotic dog demonstration led by his Ph.D. student Runxiang Jin.

Session highlights

Students engaged with current work on human-robot collaboration and asked thoughtful questions throughout the session. The live robotic dog demo helped connect advanced research ideas to a memorable in-person learning experience.

Special thanks to Prof. Rui Liu and Runxiang Jin for sharing their time, expertise, and inspiration with the course.

Lecture value for students

  • Shows how AI topics are being discussed beyond one classroom
