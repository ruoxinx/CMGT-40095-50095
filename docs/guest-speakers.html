Featured invited speaker

Prof. Rui Liu

Kent State University College of Aeronautics and Engineering

Guest lecture date: March 25, 2025

AI-Powered Robot Cognition Modeling for Trustworthy Human-Robot Collaboration

Prof. Liu shared research on trust, intent, and agility modeling in robotics, and the session included a live robotic dog demonstration led by his Ph.D. student Runxiang Jin.

Profile: https://www.kent.edu/cae/profile/rui-liu-phd

Session highlights

The session covered research on human-robot collaboration and included a live robotic dog demonstration.

Thanks to Prof. Rui Liu and Runxiang Jin for visiting the course and speaking with the students.

Why this talk was included

  • Connects course topics to current research
  • Shows one example of robotics work related to AI
  • Gives students a chance to ask questions outside the course materials
