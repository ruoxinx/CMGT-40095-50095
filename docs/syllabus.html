Course description

AI in Construction introduces practical artificial intelligence workflows for construction, architecture, and the built environment through guided notebooks, datasets, sensor-based exercises, safety vision examples, and student projects.

Who the course is for

  • Construction management students
  • Architecture and engineering learners
  • Students interested in digital workflows for the built environment
  • Learners who may be new to coding but want applied AI experience

Learning objectives

  • Interpret built-environment datasets and communicate findings visually
  • Prepare and clean construction-related data for analysis
  • Apply introductory machine learning and deep learning methods to domain-specific problems
  • Understand how sensing, computer vision, and digital twins connect to construction practice
  • Explore generative AI workflows for design communication and scenario exploration
  • Use Google Colab to run notebook-based learning activities
  • Interpret AI tools critically in relation to safety, operations, and project delivery
  • Develop and present project-based work grounded in real built-environment questions

Instructional modules

Module 1

Data Visualization and Visual Analytics for the Built Environment

Exploratory charts, trend analysis, and evidence-based visual communication for construction datasets.

Module 2

Environmental Sensing and Site Monitoring Systems

Sensor-based workflows for monitoring environmental conditions and field performance.

Module 3

Construction Data Preprocessing and Quality Control

Cleaning, structuring, and validating project data before analysis and modeling.

Module 4

Predictive Modeling for Construction Materials and Permitting

Machine learning workflows for concrete performance and permitting datasets.

Module 5

Computer Vision for Construction Safety Analytics

Image-based methods for PPE detection, safety interpretation, and jobsite awareness.

Module 6

Digital Twin Systems and Immersive Simulation

Interactive digital environments, simulation, and twin concepts for built assets.

Module 7

Generative Models for Design Communication

Diffusion-based image generation workflows for concept development and design communication.
