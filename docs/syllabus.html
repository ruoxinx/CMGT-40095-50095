Course description

AI in Construction covers notebooks, datasets, sensing exercises, computer vision examples, and student project work related to construction and the built environment.

Who the course is for

  • Construction management students
  • Architecture and engineering learners
  • Students interested in digital workflows for the built environment
  • Learners who may be new to coding but want applied AI experience

Learning objectives

  • Interpret built-environment datasets and communicate findings visually
  • Prepare and clean construction-related data for analysis
  • Apply introductory machine learning and deep learning methods to domain-specific problems
  • Understand how sensing, computer vision, and digital twins connect to construction practice
  • Study generative AI workflows for design communication and scenario exploration
  • Use Google Colab to run notebook-based learning activities
  • Interpret AI tools critically in relation to safety, operations, and project delivery
  • Develop and present project-based work grounded in real built-environment questions
