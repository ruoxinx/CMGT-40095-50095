Contributing

How to contribute to the course materials

Contributions are welcome from instructors, researchers, students, and practitioners who want to improve the course website, module pages, datasets, guest content, or teaching materials.

Ways to contribute

  • improve module pages or notebook instructions
  • contribute datasets, examples, or case studies
  • suggest accessibility and usability fixes
  • share guest lecture ideas or public resources

Contribution workflow

  1. Fork the repository.
  2. Create a focused branch.
  3. Make your update.
  4. Open a pull request with a short explanation.

Questions

For collaboration ideas, teaching reuse, or contribution questions, please get in touch.
